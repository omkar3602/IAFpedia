The IAFpedia Story

Hello, Welcome to Indian Armed Forces pedia.So glad you're here.

We are the students of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology,Pune.


Recently we have realised that there is no any userfriendly website available
which contains absolute information about exams and interveiws conducted by
our Indian Armed Forces. India having rich and glorious defence history is
unexplored by youths. Many Youngsters in INDIA are unaware of many Defence exams
and interveiws organized by IAF. The country needs young and clever aspirants in Defence sector.

-- Our Mission --

Creating awareness in youngsters about importance of Defence sector.

Inspiring the students by presenting rich and glorious history of Indian Army, Navy, AirForce.

Providing absolute information about all Defence exams and interveiws.
